Some Equipment May be Required

Some people call me Digital.
I am a Creative Director & Executive Producer. I'm still in the terminal and I get down in the metal too.

Lately, I've been playing with hardware when I'm not designing. Raspberry Pi and Arduino micro computing. I'm interested in learing new languages like Python.

My last adventure was as a Senior Produer and Prototype Engineer for a media corporation creating VR and AR applications and experiences for its subscribers and readership base.

For a short interim period, I was the Vice President and Director of Production for a small startup. That company focused on fan engagement 'activation' that entailed custom fabrication, custom software development, and creative design for user interfaces. Very nuts to bolts. Very D.I.Y.

I started as a tinkerer, a graphic designer. I love motion graphics and data viz. I'd like to be John Connor good with the hardware and software.
